About Valkyrie

My artistic journey began at the tender age of 4. A self-taught artist, I found inspiration in anime and manga, particularly the mesmerizing world of Sailor Moon. Fate intervened when I mistook an astrology book for an astronomy one, which led me into the world of spirituality, a journey I have embraced ever since. In addition to my artistic endeavors, I specialize in tarot/ oracle readings, Akashic Records reading and Theta healing. I invite you to explore my Instagram and Youtube accounts (@theslightlychippedmoon). Your support is deeply appreciated, whether through the resonance of my creations or by joining me on this magical journey through Paypal or by acquiring one of my decks from Make Playing Cards.
